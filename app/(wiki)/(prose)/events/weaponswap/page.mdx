# Weapon Swap Event

The Weapon Swap Event was a limited time event that lasted for a couple of hours. It began on <TimeLink time={new Date("2023-11-13T07:00:00+11:00").getTime()} />.
This event ran on the normal map, and had no special logo or graphics.

# Gameplay

In this event, your weapon is swapped randomly every time you get a kill. You could get any weapon, including normally unobtainable weapons,
including the [USAS-12](/weapons/guns/usas12) and the [G17 Scoped](/weapons/guns/s_g17), but not developer weapons.
